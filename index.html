Stacker Arcade

How to Play

  • Stack blocks on top of each other by timing your clicks
  • Start with 3 blocks, reduces to 2 at level 4, and 1 at level 10
  • Blocks move faster with each level
  • Miss completely and the game is over!

Prizes

  • Minor Prize (100 points) at level 11
  • Major Prize (1,000,000 points) at level 15

Controls

  • Click "Start" or press Space to begin
  • Click "Stop" or press Space to place blocks
